Han Gallery

Story of our gallery

Founded in 2001 by the Vancouver Asian Community, Han Gallery has been the bridge between the cultures. Our aim is to disseminate the East Asian culture in Vancouver. Han Gallery seeks to make a rich contribution to the cultural life of Vancouver and the surrounding cities. Be sure to take advantage of our rich resources. This year we once again strive to offer a formidable range of arts, programs, and more.
Please take the opportunity to venture into the Art Gallery and explore thoughtful and engaging exhibitions by the esteemed artists whose works we’re presenting this year.
